Status Manufacturing

Introducing StatuS Manufacturing from Visdata

StatuS Manufacturing - Our flagship ERP/MRP system, is a powerful yet simple to use, production control and management information tool providing a seamless link from Estimating through Production Control to Despatch and Invoicing.

Is It for you?

StatuS manufacturing is a fully integrated manufacturing package specifically designed to meet the needs of batch manufacturers of assemblies, components and/or finished product.

What is it and how does it work?

The system allows the user to develop and maintain an accurate estimate, which is then used to generate a quotation for the client. The estimate includes all of the elements of the job, including material specification, manufacturing route etc., and will provide alternative batch prices. Once accepted by the client, the estimate is converted into a job cost planning sheet and route card. This data forms the foundation for the fully functional and comprehensive production control system. The result of this is that the user is ensured of manufacturing for profit.

The Benefits

  • Developed with batch manufacturers over twenty years
  • Mirrors your existing way of working
  • Assists with the effective management of resources
  • Helps control costs
  • Assists with production planning and control
  • Provides comprehensive history
  • Links to accounting, payroll, data capture, quality and other systems.

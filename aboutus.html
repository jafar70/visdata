About Us

Experience Beautiful


We create software for manufacturing companies

Visdata supply software solutions to help manufacturing companies who want to satisfy their customers’ needs, grow their business and take advantage of new opportunities through increased productivity.

How we work

Typically Visdata work with ambitious owners and managers who want their business to stand out from and out perform other manufacturers. Amongst other things we find we have been most effective in helping them when

  • They have invested in new machines and equipment and want to ensure they get the best utilisation from those resources.
  • They are trying to be proactive in preventing machine, supplier and delivery problems.
  • They want to be able to react quickly to changes and are looking for software that helps them make accurate and timely decisions.

Visdata have walked around 100's of factory floors over the years and although software is our specialism we can bring a wealth of manufacturing experience and stories to help our clients achieve their goals.

History

Visdata Ltd is the result of a merger between two highly successful IT companies. Our emphasis is strictly on quality, ease of use & delivery of relevant information in an efficient and timely manner.

The Benfield Software Solutions & Gould Group International merger in 2012 further strengthens our position as a modern forward thinking “Business IT Solution Provider” and increases our wealth of IT business experience.

Sound Business Principles of Delivering:

  • Relevant Timely Data
  • In the most suitable format
  • To the appropriate people
